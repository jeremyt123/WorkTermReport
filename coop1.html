Work Term 1 - Blueprint Agencies - Web Developer

May 4th - Dec 25th


Information About The Employer

Blueprint Agencies is a marketing/digital agency that provides website, marketing, and promotional services to companies. They have worked with clients small and large all over Canada and even some international clients. Some of our notable clients include: Modern Sales co-op, Brant inStore, Slacan, Grand Erie District School Board, OCTE


My Goals

Since my co-op was 8 months I had to create 2 sets of goals.

The goals for my first 4 months were

  1. Get better with SQL
  2. Learn more about how the business runs
  3. Get faster at diagnosing and solving website issues
The goals for my second 4 months were
  1. Continue getting better at SQL (Learning more advanced queries)
  2. Get better at explaning technical information to clients
  3. Continue getting faster at diagnosing and solving website issues
